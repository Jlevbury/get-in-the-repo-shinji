James' picks

A fan of kaiju, mechs, and cel animation that killed animators, I have to recommend anime of the late 80's/early 90's titles such as Bubblegum Crisis, Dominion Tank Police, Patlabor and Gundam:zeta and double zeta additionally this era of anime is incomplete without sticking your toes in the waters of Macross / robotech. These pioneering series brought many of the common animation techniques seen in modern anime into existence, (Ex.Itano Circus:Missle Massacre).

With the glut of manga available to read theres tons of choices available, I can recommend Chainsaw Man, in case you've been living under a rock. Or a completed series such as Dorohedoro , if a grimey world of wizards, slapstick humor and the occasional beheading suit you.
